## Shortcuts

|------ |--------------- |

## File formats

- Markdown files: md;
- Image files: png, jpg, jpeg, gif, bmp, svg;
- Audio files: mp3, webm, wav, m4a, ogg, 3gp, flac;
- Video files: mp4, webm, ogv;
- PDF files: pdf.

<https://help.obsidian.md/Advanced+topics/Accepted+file+formats>

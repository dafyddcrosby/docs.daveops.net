## iOS

* PWA started in iOS 11.3+


### Capabilities

* Geolocation
* Sensors (Magnetometer, Accelerometer, Gyroscope)
* Camera
* Audio output
* Speech Synthesis (with headsets connected only)
* Apple Pay
* WebAssembly, WebRTC, WebGL as well as many other experimental features under a flag.
